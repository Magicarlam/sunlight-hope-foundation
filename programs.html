Real Impact Story

Irene's story

Irene Inyangala lives with her elderly grandmother, who struggles to support her due to limited resources.

She studies using a kerosene lamp but often lacks oil. Their home leaks during rain, and she lacks basic school items like shoes and supplies.

We provided essential supplies and a school uniform-helping her stay in school.
